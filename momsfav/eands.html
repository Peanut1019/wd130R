Entree: Tacos Al Pastor

  • 5 lds. Pork
  • 2 tbsp. Chili Powder
  • 1 tbsp. Oregano
  • 1 tbsp. Salt
  • 3/4 cup White Vinegar
  • 3 tbsp. Achiote Paste
  • 1 tbsp. Garlic Powder
  • 1 tbsp. Cumin
  • 1 tbsp. Pepper
  • 1 cup Pineapple Juice
  • 1 Fresh Pineapple or canned
  1. Skin and slice the pineapple into 1 inch rounds
  2. Preheat oven to 350*F
  3. Slice pork into 1/2 inch slices, put into large dish or bowl
  4. In another bowl, combine the spices, achiote paste, vinegar, and pineapple juice. Stir until there are not lumps.
  5. Pour mixture over the pork and toss them until they are cover on all sides.
  6. Cover bowl and refidgerat for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  7. Layer pork and pineapple in a large pan and bake for around 2 hours or until well done.
  8. Serve with Tortillas, Onions, Cilantro, Jalape√±os, Limes, and Salsa

Side: Party Perfect Potatoes

  • 6 Medium Potatoes, peeled
  • 1/2 cup Butter
  • 1 can Cream of Chicken Soup
  • 1 cup Sour Cream
  • 1/3 cup Green Onions, chopped
  • 1 cup Cheddar Cheese, grated
  • 1/2 cup Corn Flakes, crushed(optional)
  1. Boil, cool, and shred the potatoes.
  2. Melt butter in saucepan and add soup, sour cream, green onions, and cheese.
  3. Mix sauce with potatoes.
  4. Pour mixture into 13in by 9in casserole dish
  5. Top with corn flakes(optional)
  6. Bake at 350 for 30-45 minutes
